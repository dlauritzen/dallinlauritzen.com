Dallin Lauritzen About Me

Essentials

Hey! I'm Dallin Lauritzen, a desktop and mobile software developer who loves writing tools that help people get stuff done. I recently graduated from Brigham Young University with a Bachelor's in Computer Science and I'm now one of the developers of ParentLink.

History

I started building websites when my high school taught basic HTML as part of its required computer curriculum. After my Dreamweaver trial expired I began shopping around and eventually decided to try writing my own. My oldest brother pointed me at Java and I taught myself programming over the next three years. I entered BYU and finally got a formal education. I learned C/C++, Python, and a ton about what computers are capable of. In the summer of 2012 I started working part-time on ParentLink's iOS app. Once I graduated in April 2013 I joined the development team full-time.
